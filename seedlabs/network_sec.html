Firewall Exploration lab

This lab has a dual learning objective: to understand the workings of firewalls and to set up a basic firewall for a network. Students will begin by implementing a stateless packet-filtering firewall that examines packets and determines whether to drop or forward them based on predefined rules. This task will provide foundational insights into firewall operations.

VPN Tunneling

This lab aims to elucidate VPN functionality, specifically focusing on transport layer-based VPNs. Through constructing a basic VPN, students will learn about its operational components, with an emphasis on tunneling technology. Unlike a full VPN setup, this lab does not cover encryption, which is addressed in a separate SEED lab.
